FAQ

Questions, answered

Is this just an MDM I have to run?+

No. There's powerful management technology under the hood, but you never touch it. BEAM is a managed service - BAYTON operates the platform on your behalf and delivers the outcome. You get a well-run estate, not another console to learn.

Which devices do you support?+

Enterprise Android and ChromeOS, built on Google's Android Management API. Whether it's field handsets, kiosks, shared tablets or knowledge-worker devices, BEAM covers the full lifecycle.

Do I need my own infrastructure or licences?+

No. The device management licence is included in the per-device price, and there's nothing for you to host, patch or maintain. That's all on BAYTON.

Do I lose visibility or control?+

Not at all. You keep oversight through reporting and a single point of contact, and you can request a full view of device status and the audit trail whenever you need it. BAYTON carries the operational load; you keep the picture.

Can you take on devices that are already enrolled?+

Yes. Existing devices can usually be migrated into BEAM as part of onboarding, alongside enrolling new ones. Where a device can't be moved - or has to stay on its current MDM - BAYTON manages it there instead, for the same price: you're paying for the management, not the platform. Either way, BAYTON scopes the transition so nothing drops out of compliance along the way.

What does it cost and how long am I tied in?+

£0.60 per device per month is the standard rate, on a 24-month term billed annually (£7.20 per device). Shorter terms - 12-month and monthly rolling - cost a little more, while longer commitments of 36 to 60 months bring it down to £0.51. Enhanced support is available on request. Get started and the price is sized to your estate.
